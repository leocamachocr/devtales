About DevTales

Where code meets storytelling

What is Dev Tales?

Dev Tales is a personal blog where software engineering meets storytelling. Rather than just sharing tutorials or code snippets, this blog explores real-world experiences, offering insights drawn from actual projects, teams, and personal journeys.

What You'll Find Here

  • Experience-Based Stories: Lessons learned from real projects, challenges, and day-to-day life as a developer — shared as relatable, engaging tales.
  • Technology Perspectives: Thoughts on emerging and established technologies, with practical takeaways and honest opinions.
  • Human-Centered Development: A celebration of how engineers create meaningful solutions empowered by modern tools like AI, creativity, collaboration, and human context.
  • For Engineers, By an Engineer: Whether you're just starting or have years of experience, this space is for you. Expect reflections, humor, and a few hard-earned lessons along the way.

About Leonardo

Hi there! I'm Leonardo Camacho, a software developer with over 21 years of experience in the Java ecosystem. I've seen our industry evolve from client/server applications to today's microservice architectures and beyond.

What drives me is continuous learning and helping others grow. I'm passionate about clean code because I've lived through the pain of maintaining messy systems. These days, I lead development teams, mentor developers, and write stories about our shared experiences.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me teaching at the University of Costa Rica, helping future engineers develop strong technical skills. My goal is simple: help others build better software, one lesson at a time.
